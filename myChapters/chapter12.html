Adding and Subtracting integers in Binary

When adding in binary, there are several outcomes. If you add 0 to 0 the result is 0. If you add 1 to 0 the result is 1. However if you add together two 1s the sum is 0 BUT you have to carry 1 over to the next column. If you add together 3 1s you carry 1 over but have a sum of 1:

Binary addition example

When subtracting in Binary, if your top number is larger than your bottom number then you carry out a normal subtraction (subtract the bottom number from the top). However if your bottom number is larger than your top number you will have to borrow a 2 from the next column.
